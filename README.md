###  About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the **School of Architecture, Southeast University (SEU)**. My academic work focuses on the intersection of architecture and technology, specifically in **Computational Design**, **Generative Design**, and **AI applications in Architecture**.

My research aims to synchronize computational technologies with design thinking. I am actively exploring data-driven workflows and typology-based urban morphology generation.

**Key Contributions:**
- **Tool Development:** Major contributor to generative tools like **[SIMForms]** and **[FLEXUrban]**.
- **Recognition:** Recipient of the 2024 **Young CAADRIA Award**.
